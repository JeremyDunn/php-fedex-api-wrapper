    • \PickupScheduleOption

    The constraints on the scheduling of a dispatch, where that dispatch would be made by the Carrier, occur on the PickupDate, and would be scheduled (created by means of a CourierDispatchRequest) on a date related to the PickupDate as described by the "ScheduleDay" (SAME_DAY meaning that the creation would occur on the PickupDate, and FUTURE_DAY meaning that the creation would occur on a date prior to the PickupDate).
